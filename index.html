THE BRAND - SherDesign

What does the company do, or offer?

SherDesign offers personalized, handmade paper greeting cards; these greeting cards are produced from recycled material and printed with a customized greeting message.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

A company aims to involve environmentally friendly paper within the innovative new ideas for communicating emotions to friends, relatives and others in the traditional method of sending a greeting card, who also cares and promotes a sustainable and greener environment.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

The essential characteristics of a company are following: commitment to socially responsible for operations and values, the closure of the loop in paper waste by using 100% recycled materials, and the operations respect the environment in order to preserve our world's resources from greenhouse gas emissions, wastewater, net energy and trees.

TARGET PERSONAS

Potential customer#1:

A 25-year-old girl who wants to make a sincere Happy Mother’s Day card for her mom, but she does not have any idea and handcrafting skills to make a handmade card. The individual is in search of a creative handmade card to show her sincereness.

Potential customer#2:

A 40-year-old environmental protester supports the companies who are committed to being 100% environmentally friendly – from products to production processes. He is currently seeking to for a loving greeting card to celebrate his 10th wedding anniversary with his wife.

TASKS

  1. Select a card type for the greeting card.
  2. Inquire about corporate orders, i.e. to whom, colour and paper choice, from who, decoration preference, and desired content through a form.
  3. Read about sourcing and artists who make the greeting card.
  4. See the gallery of samples organized from user-generated content on social media, i.e. Instagram, Facebook, or Twitter.

Due to the purpose of the greeting card is to deliver the happiness and joy through traditional cards, I decided to use a bright colour theme to express the positive atmosphere for this website. The matcha green and grass green the first two colour counts from right-hand-side are the primary colours in which they are applied on the navigation section and the first two headings. The bright green in the middle is for highlighting the specific words to catch the reader's attention, i.e quotes or code. The bright yellow is for the background colour of the link text when a link is hovered. In this case, the readers can visualize clearly that he/she is hovering the link. The light yellow on the very left is for the background color of the text box, when he/she is clicked on a particular textbox, such as for entering his/her name or comments.
